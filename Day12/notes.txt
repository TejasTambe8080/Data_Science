60–70% time spent here
🧹 PART 2 — DATA CLEANING TECHNIQUES
🔹 1. Handle Missing Values
df.isnull()
df.dropna()
df.fillna(0)
🔹 2. Remove Duplicates
df.drop_duplicates()
🔹 3. Rename Columns
df.rename(columns={"old": "new"}, inplace=True)
🔹 4. Change Data Type
df["marks"] = df["marks"].astype(int)
🔹 5. String Cleaning
df["name"] = df["name"].str.strip().str.lower()
📊 PART 3 — GROUPBY (VERY IMPORTANT)
🔹 What is groupby()?

👉 Group data by category

Example:
df.groupby("department")["salary"].mean()

👉 Used in:

Business analytics
Reports
Dashboards
📊 PART 4 — AGGREGATION
🔹 Functions:
df["marks"].mean()
df["marks"].sum()
df["marks"].max()
df["marks"].min()
🔹 Multiple Aggregations
df.groupby("dept")["salary"].agg(["mean", "max", "min"])
