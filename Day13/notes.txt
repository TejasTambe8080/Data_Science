🧠 PART 2 — EDA CHECKLIST (VERY IMPORTANT)

Whenever you see a dataset, ask:

✅ Basic Info
How many rows & columns?
What are data types?
df.shape
df.info()
✅ Statistical Summary
df.describe()
✅ Missing Values
df.isnull().sum()
✅ Unique Values
df["column"].nunique()
df["column"].value_counts()
✅ Relationships
df.groupby("category")["value"].mean()
